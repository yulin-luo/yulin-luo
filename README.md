<h2 align="center">Hi, I am Yulin Luo</h2>

<p align="center">
  PhD student at Peking University, working on generalizable embodied foundation models for the open world.
</p>

      <h4>Bio</h4>
      <p>
        I am a PhD student at the School of Computer Science, Peking University, advised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang.
        I received my B.Eng. in Automation from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2023.
      </p>

      <h4>Research Interests</h4>
      <p>
        My research focuses on <b>generalizable embodied foundation models for the open world</b>,
        especially models and data that generalize across objects, skills, embodiments, scenes, and tasks.
      </p>
      <p>
        I am also interested in the co-evolution of models and data for scaling embodied intelligence from
        narrow benchmarks to real-world generalization.
      </p>

      <h4>Current Directions</h4>
      <ul>
        <li>Embodied AI, VLA models, and robot foundation models</li>
        <li>Open-world embodied evaluation and benchmark design</li>
        <li>Agentic/self-evolving embodied systems</li>
        <li>Data-centric learning and generalization under distribution shift</li>
      </ul>
